"To travel is to discover that everyone is wrong about other countries." - Aldous Huxley

The terms “home sweet home” and “deep in the heart of Texas” will always resonate with me, but it is time to add another aphorism to my vocabulary, hopefully in Hindi. I am under the impression that the adolescents of the world attend school up until the 12th grade or that everyone binge-watches shows on Netflix. This is inaccurate because there exist major differences in educational systems all over the world, and not everyone knows about or has access to Netflix.

I would also gain valuable insights on the multinational collaborative planning that allows for cost-effective software development, which would bridge the gap between school and a career in software engineering, and serve as a launching pad towards reaching my career goals of working with educational technology.

Besides taking a class and going on company visits to Accenture and MindTree, I will also have the chance to go white water rafting at the Bheemeshwari Adventure and Nature Camp or zorbing (see picture above with plastic ball) at Della Adventure, one of India’s largest adventure parks. It would also be an awesome experience to come back to the United States knowing you have मित्र (friends) on the other side of the world.
